Camping Training, September 18 2021

This course qualifies participants to lead girls on camping trips at established primitive campsites where the base campsite cannot be farther than two miles from the point-of-origin. Skill building includes health and safety; water purification; using lightweight, inexpensive gear; low impact procedures and other skills needed for primitive camping.

Game Design, September 19 2021
